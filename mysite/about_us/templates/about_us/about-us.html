About Us

Entrepreneurship development cell promotes, harnesses and catalyses the knowledge or technology based start-ups through young and innovative ideas of the students. With the main motive of evolving developers and innovators, the E-cell strives to assist every aspiring entrepreneur at every single step in their journey, right from the initiative of conceiving to the successful launching of a viable product. EDC builds the capacity and willingness to develop, organise, and manage a business venture along with risks in order to make a profit. Numerous events are organised regularly to explore the talent and enhance the skill of the students.The events induce motivation and competence among young prospective entrepreneurs. Prizes are awarded to the better ones and inspire others to work harder and bring out the best in them. All in all, EDC aims to stimulate the students for adopting entrepreneurship as a career and to make themselves able enough to identify and exploit the opportunities successfully for a new venture.
